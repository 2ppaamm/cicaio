Welcome to LEAPFROGGER INSTITUTE.

The most comprehensive creative arts curriculum in the world.

LeapFrogger LLC offers educational solutions for the development of social skills and media competency in the modern world. Utilizing physical facilities and a robust online curriculum, we offer a comprehensive arts and media education, operating in over 200 franchise locations in 40 countries worldwide.

At LeapFrogger, we provide students with a university-caliber education on everything from video production and photography to graphics and social media entrepreneurship. Our program also offers options for building English and Mandarin language proficiency. LeapFrogger offers a carefully designed curriculum that builds real-world skills and knowledge along a rational and structured path.

We offer the highest level of experience across a streaming platform so students can access our comprehensive curriculum from anywhere. We understand that modern media is flexible, so we teach the uses of everything from available technologies and smartphones to cutting edge cinematic hardware and software.

Sometimes, a great solution uncovers another problem...

As children embrace social media technologies, the reliance on it can rob them of important opportunities in building social skills. That’s why LeapFrogger owns and operates a massive global network of youth-based performance arts academies. At all of our 200+ locations in 40 countries worldwide, we offer after school and summer programs, teaching kids social skills and confidence through performance arts instruction.

VIDEOGRAPHY AND PHOTOGRAPHY

ANIMATION AND GRAPHIC DESIGN FOR FILM AND SOCIAL MEDIA

Introductory and advanced courses in Adobe Photoshop and After Effects. Learn to create stunning graphics and animations for your films, social media sites, marketing platforms or just for fun!

Wide variety of courses available for social media marketing, management, writing, branding and responsible use.
